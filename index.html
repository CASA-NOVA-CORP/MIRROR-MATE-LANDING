How It Works

1

Pair the devices

Host creates a PIN and a short pairing code. Partner enters the code on their phone to link devices.

2

Choose what to block

Host sees a real-time app list from the mirrored device and toggles which apps should be blocked.

3

Enforcement is silent

On the mirrored device, blocked apps simply fail to open — even if the device goes offline.
